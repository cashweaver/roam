#+title: Sama Vritti Pranayama
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+author: Cash Weaver
#+date: [2022-01-31 Mon 10:32]

A four-part breathing technique:

1. Inhale
2. Hold
3. Exhale
4. Hold

The practitioner should perform each part for the same duration (eg: inhale four seconds, hold four seconds, etc).
